Somerville Survivors Fantasy League

    Fantasy League Rules

    Submit your picks every week to score points in the following categories:

    1) Weekly Main Questions

    QuestionPre-Merge or GroupPost-Merge or Individual
    Who will win reward?510
    Who will win immunity?515
    Who will be eliminated?1020
    Who will be safe?1010

    Before the merge points for reward and immunity will be assessed per tribe, so simply pick any player on the tribe you predict will win that challenge.


    2) Weekly Bonus Questions

    QuestionPre-Merge or GroupPost-Merge or Individual
    Who says the episode title?24
    Will an immunity idol or secret advantage be found?24
    Will an immunity idol or secret advantage be played?24
    Will someone play their Shot-in-the-Dark?24
    Will the vote be unanimous?24
    Who (if anyone) will go on a journey?24
    Will there be nudity?24
    Will someone catch a fish?24
    Who will have the most confessionals?24
    Will someone visibly cry?24

    And other bonus questions that may vary by season!

    Questions may be added for weekly challenges (who wins the fire making challeng) or to accomodate for specifics within the season (who is sent to Ghost Island this week?).


    3) Final Three

    Players will pick who they think will be the Final Three survivors prior to the second episode.

    For each survivor chosen that makes it to the Final Three they will recieve +30 points (max 90).


    4) Final Eight

    When there are 8 Survivors left, weekly points will no longer be assigned for the main "Eliminated" and "Safe" questions. Bonus questions will continue to count.

    Instead, players will rank the remaining players in the order they go home. Based on these picks, points will be assigned based on how close each pick was to the actual order.

    A perfect prediction for the Final Eight recieves +140 points.
